About Us

IND fitness is a fully equipped gym including cardio equipment, free weights and plate loaded machines ideal for allowing you to have an all over body workout,no matter what age or fitness level you are at.

Cardio

Our cardio area includes treadmills,cross trainers and many more for those who like to burn calories and build up a sweat. The studio area has plenty of room for warming up whether that be using the punch bags and pads or exercise balls and kettlebells,or simply just stretching.

Free Weights

As well as our cardio area we also have a large range of dumbells and barbells for those who prefer free weights.These are suitable for all levels of experience. Using the dumbells along with our benches,both incline and flat will help when targeting specific muscle groups.

Strength Equipment

With over 30 cable,plate and pin loaded machines IND fitness has one of the biggest ranges of equipment in the area,offering an extensive workout for all.
